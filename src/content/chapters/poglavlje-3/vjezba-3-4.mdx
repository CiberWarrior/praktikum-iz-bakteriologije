## Vježba 3.4 - Bojenje bakterija po Gramu

<div style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #e8f4fd 0%, #d1ecf1 100%); border-radius: 15px; padding: 25px; margin: 20px 0;">
  
  <div style="background: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 20px; border-left: 4px solid #2196f3;">
    <div style="display: flex; align-items: center; margin-bottom: 15px;">
      <span style="font-size: 20px; margin-right: 10px;">🎯</span>
      <h3 style="margin: 0; color: #1565c0; font-size: 1.3rem; font-weight: 600;">Cilj vježbe</h3>
    </div>
    <p style="margin: 0; color: #2d3748; line-height: 1.6;">
      Savladavanje tehnike složenog diferencijalnog bojenja po Gramu za razlikovanje Gram-pozitivnih i Gram-negativnih bakterija.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div style="background: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 20px; border-left: 4px solid #ff9800;">
    <div style="display: flex; align-items: center; margin-bottom: 15px;">
      <span style="font-size: 20px; margin-right: 10px;">🧪</span>
      <h3 style="margin: 0; color: #f57c00; font-size: 1.3rem; font-weight: 600;">Materijali i reagensi</h3>
    </div>
    <ul style="margin: 0; padding-left: 20px; color: #2d3748;">
      <li>Pripremljeni bakterijski preparat (fiksiran na plamenu)</li>
      <li>Kristal violet</li>
      <li>Lugolova otopina (zasićena otopina joda u kalijevu jodidu)</li>
      <li>96%-tni etanol</li>
      <li>Razrijeđeni karbol fuksin ili safranin</li>
      <li>Voda iz vodovoda</li>
      <li>Imerziono ulje</li>
      <li>Mikroskop s imerzionim objektivom</li>
      <li>Pinceta</li>
      <li>Kadica za bojenje</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div style="background: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 20px; border-left: 4px solid #4caf50;">
    <div style="display: flex; align-items: center; margin-bottom: 15px;">
      <span style="font-size: 20px; margin-right: 10px;">📋</span>
      <h3 style="margin: 0; color: #388e3c; font-size: 1.3rem; font-weight: 600;">Postupak</h3>
    </div>
    <ol style="margin: 0; padding-left: 20px; color: #2d3748;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Na osušeni preparat <strong>fiksiran na plamenu</strong> nanijeti <strong>kristal violet</strong></li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Nakon <strong>3 minute</strong> odliti (ne ispirati vodom)</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Preliti <strong>Lugolovom otopinom</strong> (zasićena otopina joda u kalijevu jodidu)</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Odmah odliti i ponovno preliti te ostaviti stajati <strong>1 minutu</strong></li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Nakon 1 min odliti sadržaj</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Predmetno stakalce s preparatom uroniti u kadicu s <strong>96%-tnim etanolom</strong></li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Pincetom izvaditi i kontrolirati prestanak izlaženja ljubičaste boje</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Djelovanje alkohola prekinuti ispiranjem tekućom vodom</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Preparat osušiti i preliti <strong>razrijeđenim karbol fuksinom ili safraninom</strong></li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Nakon <strong>30 sekundi</strong> isprati tekućom vodom</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Obojeni preparat osušiti između filter-papira ili na zraku</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 0;">Na preparat dodati kap imerzionog ulja i mikroskopirati pod imerzionim objektivom</li>
    </ol>
  </div>

  <div style="background: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 20px; border-left: 4px solid #9c27b0;">

    </div>
    <p style="margin: 0 0 15px 0; color: #2d3748; line-height: 1.6;">
      Metodom bojenja po Gramu:
    </p>
    <ul style="margin: 0; padding-left: 20px; color: #2d3748;">
      <li><strong>Gram-pozitivne bakterije</strong> oboje se <strong>plavo</strong></li>
      <li><strong>Gram-negativne bakterije</strong> oboje se <strong>crveno</strong></li>
    </ul>
